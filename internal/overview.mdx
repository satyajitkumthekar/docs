The second part is purely mental. It is the ability to do what you know you have to do, consistently.

And this is where fitness gets all the hype. Because in building the skill to consistently do what you're supposed to do, you have to solve a long tail of internal knots and beliefs.

These are the same mental knots and beliefs that act as the base skills to become successful in any domain of life.
